# Resonance - Client Requirements

## Project Context

**Client:** Josh / AI West (building for personal use + productization)
**Primary Use Case:** Create visuals for AI West YouTube videos (organic/ambient music)
**Secondary Use Case:** Launch as SaaS product for organic musicians

## Client's Technical Profile

- **Not a developer:** Cannot fix code issues
- **Comfortable with tools:** Can use dashboards, settings, upload files
- **Workflow preference:** Upload → configure → generate → download

## Audio Input Flexibility

**Required formats:** MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A
**Duration:** 30 seconds to 10 minutes (initial), up to 30 minutes (future)

## Visual Aesthetic Focus

**Strongly prefer:**
- Organic, flowing, natural aesthetics
- Botanical growth patterns
- Fluid dynamics
- Particle systems (gentle, not chaotic)
- Light and shadow play
- Painterly effects

**Avoid:**
- Digital glitches
- Harsh strobes
- Aggressive electronic visuals
- Mechanical, robotic aesthetics

## Music Genre Expectations

**Primary genres:**
- Ambient instrumental
- Acoustic singer-songwriter
- Folk music
- Classical/chamber music
- Jazz (smooth)
- Meditation/wellness audio

## Output Requirements

**Video specs:**
- Codec: H.264
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Format: MP4 (YouTube compatible)

**Quality tiers:**
- Free: 720p, watermarked
- Creator: 1080p, no watermark
- Pro: Up to 4K
- Studio: 4K + source files

## Success Metrics

**For personal use:**
- 3+ videos per week for YouTube
- Each video unique and on-brand
- Saves 30+ hours per month

**For product:**
- 100+ users in first 3 months
- 20+ paying customers in 6 months
- 4.5+ star rating
